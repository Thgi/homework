About Me

I find design inspiration from art everywhere. I never stop coming up with new designs. I want to use my art to communicate my passion for art visually. I love using text, images, photography, and illustrations to deliver the message to my audience. I want to be a graphic designer to face and conquer new challenges. I strongly believe to become a great graphic designer, you have to be good at using software, but also be good at imagining possibilities.
